Brief Biography

The Blue Scholars are a Seattle-based hip-hop duo. Their lyrics revolve around the themes of social justice, Seattle pride, and their lives as musicians.

Their latest album, Cinemetropolis, was released in 2011. Each song is named after a celebrity in the music or film industry, celebrating their contributions to society.

Joe Metro

"Joe Metro" is one of the band's more popular songs. It features the lead vocalist, MC Geologic, taking a bus in his area and noticing the people of his neighborhood.
